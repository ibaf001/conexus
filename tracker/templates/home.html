About

A small business offering Civil Engineering services. They have 15- 20 clients of different sizes. It employs 15-20 employees, 8 of them are Civil engineers while 6 works in the field. The Engineers work in the office on data analysis and calculations and the workers in the field use that information to perform different tasks. The workload is tracked and assigned using SS and several operations tasks/communications are done via email threads. The company is unable to easily keep track of projects due dates, resources bottlenecks or even employee efficiencies/productivity. The team manager spends the majority of the time following up on different tasks as well as resource management. The company is growing and the current manual processes are not sustainable nor are they efficient.
